Servers ng telcos, bumigay sa unang araw ng SIM card registration

Nagkaroon ng problema sa server portals ng mga telecommunication companies sa unang araw ng pagrerehistro ng subscriber identity module (SIM) cards. Sinimulan ang SIM card registration ngayong araw, December 27, 2022. Alas sais y media pa lang ng umaga, ang registration link para sa Globe ay nagpapakita na ng "Bad gateway" at hinihiling sa subscribers to "try again later." Pagsapit ng 10:30 a.m., idinidirekta na ng link ang subscribers sa FAQ page. May mga naka-access naman sa Smart portal, pero matagal bago mag-load ang link o kaya ay ayaw talagang mag-load. Ang mga DITO subscribers ay nakapag-register sa pamamagitan ng DITO app. Gayunpaman, may ilang subscribers na matagumpay na nakapag-register sa lahat ng links mula 4:00 a.m. hanggang 5:00 a.m.

NTC to public: Report spam messages

The National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) has asked the public to immediately report suspicious text messages as phishing scams continue to proliferate. NTC has come up with steps to protect the public from scammers. These include identifying a text scam, ignoring and reporting it, and then blocking the number and deleting the message.

SIM registration: Digital advocates ask telcos, gov’t to ensure data privacy

Digital advocates urged public telecommunications entities (PTE) to make sure that all SIM will be registered and that no data will be compromised. “We are hoping that the telcos have prepared for this day and have employed steps to ensure that the registration process will be easy. NTC sa telcos: Bigyang babala ang subscribers kontra text scam

MANILA, Philippines — Muling inatasan ng National Telecommunications Commission (NTC) ang telecommunications companies na bigyang babala ang kanilang mga subscri­bers laban sa paglaganap ng text scam na ngayon ay mayroon ng pangalan ng recipient. Nakasaad sa memorandum ng NTC sa network service providers na Globe Telecoms, Smart Communication at Dito Telecommunity na bigyang babala ang mga subscribers hinggil sa text scam sa petsang mula August 31 hanggang September 6, 2022. Ang nilalaman ng babala ay dapat na “Babala. Huwag pong maniwala sa text na naglalaman ng inyong pangalan na nag-aalok ng trabaho, pabuya o pera. Ito po ay isang scam”

About us

When acquiring a SIM card, customers must supply a list of identifying details to licensed resellers and merchants of SIM cards. These shops will be trained to go through a strict verification procedure in order to ensure that the SIM Card Registration Law is implemented completely and without any gaps.
